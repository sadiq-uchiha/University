About IGCE - College

Indiragandhi College Of Education is one of the best B. Ed. College in the Chitradurga District. It came into existence in the year 2005 under S. Nijalingappa Educational and Rural Development Trust (R.) Kuvempu Nagara, Hosadurga. The College is co-educational by its nature. The college has a sum of 4 acres of land and the college premises is build within 716 square meters.


Indiragandhi College of Education, Kuvempu Nagar, Hosadurga situated at Hosadurga Taluk, Chitradurga District, Karnataka is one of the well known College which is affiliated to Davanagere University and recognized by NCTE. An educational college is meant for the complete physical, intellectual, emotional, social, moral and spiritual development of student teachers. This college is making steady progress in developing and providing the best environment for teacher education. This college equipped with the highly qualified and most experienced Principal and Staff members. Every year teacher students of this college are getting qualified in the top 10 university ranking list.
